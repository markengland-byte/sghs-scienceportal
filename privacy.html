1. Overview

The SGHS Learning Portal ("the Portal") is an educational platform operated by Southern Gap High School ("the School") for use by enrolled students and authorized teaching staff. This policy describes what student data is collected, why it is collected, how it is protected, and who can access it.

The Portal is designed to comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), which protects the privacy of student education records.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No student ID numbers, Social Security numbers, or government identifiers
  • No home addresses, phone numbers, or personal contact information
  • No photographs, videos, or biometric data
  • No location data or device identifiers
  • No cookies or third-party advertising trackers

3. How Data Is Used

Student data is used exclusively for educational purposes:

  • Grading: Teachers view quiz scores and responses to assess student understanding
  • Analytics: Teachers identify which topics students struggle with to adjust instruction
  • Progress tracking: Students resume lessons where they left off
  • Classroom management: Class code system ensures data is associated with the correct teacher and period

Student data is never used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any non-educational purpose.

5. Data Storage & Security

Where Data Is Stored

Security Measures

  • Database access control: Row Level Security (RLS) ensures each teacher can only access their own class data
  • Teacher authentication: Teacher dashboard requires Supabase Auth login (email + password)
  • HTTPS everywhere: All data transmission is encrypted via TLS
  • Security headers: Content Security Policy, X-Frame-Options, Strict-Transport-Security, and other headers protect against common web attacks
  • Subresource Integrity: Third-party scripts are verified with SRI hashes to prevent tampering
  • Audit logging: Teacher data access is logged for accountability
  • No server-side storage of passwords: Teacher passwords are hashed; student access uses class codes (no passwords)

7. Data Retention

  • Student data is retained for the current school year plus one additional year for academic record purposes
  • After the retention period, data is permanently deleted from the database
  • Browser localStorage data expires automatically after 30 days or can be cleared by the student at any time
  • Teachers can request early deletion of specific student data through the school administrator

8. FERPA Rights

Under FERPA, parents and eligible students (age 18+) have the right to:

  • Inspect and review the student's education records maintained by the Portal
  • Request amendment of records believed to be inaccurate or misleading
  • Consent to disclosure of personally identifiable information (except in cases where FERPA authorizes disclosure without consent, such as to school officials with legitimate educational interest)

To exercise these rights, contact the school administration using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Material changes will be communicated via the Portal's main page. The "Effective" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.
